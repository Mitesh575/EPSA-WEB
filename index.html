About EPSA

Your trusted partner for academic journals and periodicals

Empowering Institutions Since 2017

Everest Periodical Subscription Agency (EPSA) is a leading subscription agency based in Chennai, India, dedicated to providing premium academic journals and periodicals to Arts and Science colleges, universities, and research institutions across the country.

With over 9 years of unwavering trust and performance, we have built strong relationships with 150+ institutions, offering a curated catalogue of 300+ journals spanning every major academic discipline — from Tamil and English literature to cutting-edge Computer Science and Biotechnology research.

We partner with renowned publishers to provide SCOPUS-indexed, UGC CARE-listed, and other peer-reviewed journals, ensuring your library receives only the highest quality academic resources at competitive prices.
